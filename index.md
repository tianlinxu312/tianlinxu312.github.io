I am a Senior Deep Learning Scientist at Illumina Artificial Intelligence Lab, working on the development of novel deep learning algorithms for deciphering the human genetic code.  Previously I completed my doctoral degree in Statistics at London School of Economics, jointly supervised by [Dr. Wicher Bergsma](https://www.lse.ac.uk/Statistics/People/Dr-Wicher-Bergsma) and [Prof. Beatrice Acciaio](http://beatrice-acciaio.net/). Before starting my PhD, I did my undergraduate study in Computer Science and masters in Mathematical Finance.
